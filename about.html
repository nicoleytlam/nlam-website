The Journey of a Musical Director

Nicole Lam is a music director, orchestral conductor, pianist, and vocalist in her last year of study at Yale University.

Recently: Musical Director of Into The Woods, produced by the Yale Dramatic Association

Current: Assistant Conductor of Yale Symphony Orchestra, Music Director of Berkeley College Orchestra, and Music Supervisor for Pippin produced by the Yale Theater, Dance, & Performance Studies Department.

Nicole is working towards her Bachelor's and Master's degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at Yale University.
