Making Music Education Accessible to All

Love Through Music is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making music education accessible to all students, regardless of their financial circumstances. Through our programs, we provide:

  • Free music lessons
  • Instrument lending program
  • Community performances
  • Mentorship opportunities
